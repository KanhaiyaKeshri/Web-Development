CONTACT CONTRACTING APP

PROBLEM STATEMENT: -

When we shift to a new flat in a new unfamiliar city, we face a lot of difficulties like household issues such as plumber related work, electricity related work, carpentry related work.
To get our work done we wait for many days or we pay huge amount.
Because of lack of information that where we will get cheap plumber or electrician, we face a lot difficulty.
Keeping all these in mind we intend to build an android app that will show the list of all the plumber,
electrician available in that particular city and we can contact them anytime.
